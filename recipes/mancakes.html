Tacos

Description

Sweet/savory hybrid mancakes can be tricky but I think this worked beautifully.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Green onions
  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Place bacon in skillet, cook over medium-high heat
  2. Whisk flour, cornmeal, baking powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper together in a large bowl
  3. Combine maple syrup and chipotle chile powder together in a small bowl; whisk until chile powder is completely dissolved.
  4. Heat 1 teaspoon bacon drippings, 1 teaspoon oil, and 1 teaspoon butter on a griddle over medium-high heat.
  5. Bon Apetit!

Tacos

Description

Follow this recipe to make homemade tacos perfect for a friday evening.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Cheese
  • Salad
  • Corn
  • Taco sauce
  • Tortilla bread
  • Taco spice mix

Steps

  1. Fry the beef in taco spice mix
  2. Grate the cheese and chop the salad if needed
  3. warm the tortilla bread in the oven on blabla
  4. Mix all ingredients in the bread
  5. Fold the bread
  6. Bon Apetit!
